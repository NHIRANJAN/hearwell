Getting Started

Welcome to our hearing app! Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Make sure you are in a quiet place.
  2. Wear the headphones.
  3. Click "Get Started" below.

Right Ear

  1. Click the "Generate Audio" button.
  2. If you hear the sound, press "YES".
  3. If not, press "NO".

Left Ear

  1. Click the "Generate audio" button.
  2. If you hear the sound, press "YES".
  3. If not, press "NO".
